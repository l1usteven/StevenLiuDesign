CLIENT

L'Oréal x Yves Saint Laurent

MY ROLE

  • Project Manager
  • UX/UI Designer

SUMMARY

Yves Saint Laurent aimed to leverage our AI foundation shade recommender to elevate their in-store experience.

THE BRIEF

BUSINESS GOALS

Leverage AI technology to enhance the in-store experience, building trust and credibility through exceptional service

PROBLEM

The current manual process which requires our engineering team to facilitate the implementation of feedback, is slowing the iteration process, resulting in significant delays and inefficiencies

RESEARCH PROCESS

METHODS USED

  • User Interviews
  • User Journey
  • Market Research Analysis
  • Prototype Testing

I conducted interviews and shadowed beauty advisors (BAs) during their skin-tone analysis sessions with in-store customers, observing their process of recommending the most suitable shades.

USER JOURNEY

KEY FOCUS AREAS

  • What makes customers more likely to trust their recommendations?
  • What kind of challenges do BAs face when recommending products?
  • What are the current challenges with collecting feedback from customers?
  • Lo-Fi Prototype feedback

USER NEEDS

CREDIBILITY

BAs need a better way to prove to customers that their recommendations are correct without just saying, "trust me".

They need an app that supplements their credibility without taking away the personal aspect of their role.

PERSONALIZATION

Not all customers trust an AI to recommend them their shade, they want to see that their recommendation was personal.

Personalization was key to help build the trust between BAs and customers. This was visualized through the app in real-time.
