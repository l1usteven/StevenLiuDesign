L'Oréal x Yves Saint Laurent

MY ROLE

  • UX/UI Designer

SUMMARY

Yves Saint Laurent aimed to leverage our AI foundation shade recommender to elevate their in-store experience.

THE BRIEF

BUSINESS GOALS

Leverage AI technology to enhance the in-store experience, building trust and credibility through exceptional service

PROBLEM

The current manual process which requires our engineering team to facilitate the implementation of feedback, is slowing the iteration process, resulting in significant delays and inefficiencies

RESEARCH PROCESS

METHODS USED

  • User Interviews
  • User Journey
  • User Flow
  • Market Research Analysis
  • Usability Testing

I conducted interviews and shadowed beauty advisors (BAs) during their skin-tone analysis sessions with in-store customers, observing their process of recommending the most suitable shades.

USER JOURNEY

USER FLOW

KEY FOCUS AREAS

  • What makes customers more likely to trust their recommendations?
  • What kind of challenges do BAs face when recommending products?
  • What are the current challenges with collecting feedback from customers?
  • Lo-Fi Prototype feedback

USER NEEDS

CREDIBILITY

BAs need a better way to prove to customers that their recommendations are correct without just saying, "trust me".

They need an app that supplements their credibility without taking away the personal aspect of their role.

PERSONALIZATION

Not all customers trust an AI to recommend them their shade, they want to see that their recommendation was personal.

Personalization was key to help build the trust between BAs and customers. This was visualized through the app in real-time.

DESIGN PROCESS

WIREFRAME

With the initial requirements for an MVP from the engineering team, I proceeded to develop wireframe sketches based on the established user flow.

The goal was to craft an interactive experience enabling users to engage with the live scan visualization. This allowed users to see how each scan affected their results in real-time. From there, they could personalize their recommendations, test them out, and provide feedback on the AI's suggestions for further enhancement.

Our competitors took a more straightforward approach by simply presenting the final recommended shades and products without much explanation. However, our research showed that customers were eager to delve deeper into the "how" and "why" behind their shade recommendations.

CONCLUSION

ACHIEVEMENTS

Improved feedback loop time by automatically delivering customer feedback on shade match quality to the engineering team, resulting in a 30% increase in workflow speed.

Increased Beauty Advisors' credibility with a comprehensive visual breakdown of their customers' shade recommendation.

LESSONS

No need to reinvent the wheel. Few users used the left sidebar, and it was not obvious it could be clicked. moving it to the top and making it more recognizable significantly increased its usage and opened up more space for the main content.
