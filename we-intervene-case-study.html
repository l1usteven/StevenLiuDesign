    Overview

    For Goodness Cakes is a custom cake business that creates scrumptious treats for all occasions. Saira, the self-taught baker, offers personalized cake creations and delivery services. She needs a landing page for her business to streamline communication with clients and manage orders efficiently.

    As a Project Manager, I supervised 3 designers and 5 developers in designing and developing a user-friendly landing page within budget and on time. The project spanned 2 months, with a 1-month design sprint followed by a 1-month implementation phase.

    Problems

    Customer Confusion

    For Goodness Cakes needs a clear method of announcing updates and a streamlined platform for clients to place orders with precise specifications to avoid any potential confusion or disorganization.

    Solutions

    Bulletin Board

    Incorporated a bulletin board that can easily be updated via a pinned Tweet.

    Custom Order Form

    Enables clients to submit intricate details so the baker can turn the client’s vision into a reality.

    Research

    Our initial research showed clients struggled most when it came to putting what they imagined onto paper. This leads to frustration since if clients cannot even articulate what they imagined, how can the baker re-create it?

    Image Upload

    6 of 11 Clients wanted to upload reference images.

    Dietary Restrictions

    5 of 11 Clients specified dietary restrictions.

    Ideation

    With the user pain points in mind, we developed How Might We statements asking ourselves:

    “How might we provide a simple method for clients to help the baker visualize their order?”

    User Flow

    To understand the complexity of different potential client orders, I created a user flow diagram that maps out how clients would navigate the site while placing an order.

    Image Upload & Dietary Restrictions

    1. Image Upload

      2. Image upload makes the ordering process effortless by enabling clients to upload reference images for their desired cake design, which both reassures them and provides the baker with a clear guide to work from.

    2. Dietary Restrictions

      3. A textbox allows clients to specify their dietary restrictions and ingredients to avoid, making it easy for the baker to accommodate their needs.

    Style Guide

    I revised the landing page by implementing a style guide following user testing of the prototype to ensure consistent color and typography. The style guide embodies the elegance of the baker's creations.

    Reflection

    Impact

    My client was highly satisfied with the outcome of their new landing page. It was expected to result in a 20% increase in orders. However, due to the impact of COVID-19 on the one-person operation of For Goodness Cakes, new orders are temporarily suspended until further notice.

    Client’s Instagram, “Temporarily closed until further notice.” (2022)

    Challenges

    For my first time working with a dedicated development team, the primary challenge I faced was related to time constraints. The majority of the developers were also employed full-time, which sometimes made coordinating schedules difficult. However, I was able to overcome this by finding opportunities to meet with the lead developer to review any necessary changes. She then communicated those changes to the rest of the team.
