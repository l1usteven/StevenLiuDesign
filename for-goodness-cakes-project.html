CLIENT

For Goodness Cakes

MY ROLE

  • Project Manager
  • UX/UI Designer

SUMMARY

For Goodness Cakes, a bespoke bakery specializing in custom cakes, wanted to optimize their communication channels and elevate the ordering experience.

THE BRIEF

BUSINESS GOALS

Develop a landing page to actively proactively engage with customers and enhance clarity of the ordering process.

PROBLEM

As a small business primarily operating on social media, posting announcements on multiple platforms creates confusion and difficulties for both parties. This complicates the ordering process, introducing the potential for errors.

RESEARCH PROCESS

METHODS USED

  • User Interviews
  • User Flow
  • Information Architecture
  • A/B Testing
  • Prototype Testing

I interviewed customers about their cake ordering experiences to create an optimized user flow.

INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

USER FLOW

USER NEEDS

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Currently, announcements and updates are posted manually on several social media platforms. This is tedious for the client as well as the customers.

A dedicated section on the landing page would make it easier for customers to keep up with business announcements.

INSPIRATION IMAGES

Customers often struggle with depicting their cake ideas in words. This often leads to unmet expectations.

Many customers want the ability to upload their inspiration for designs and styles to help the baker.
